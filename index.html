About The Owner NotHouse

Hey! I'm NotHouse, the brain behind Neptune Discord. My coding journey started with JavaScript and Python, and pretty quickly, I got hooked on building cool stuff for the Discord community.

I've been working on bots and Discord integrations for over 5 years now, fine-tuning my skills to create tools that really level up the Discord experience. Whether it's the Proof Bot that makes hilarious fake images for fun or secure authentication and blockchain tools, I'm all about pushing the limits of what's possible in Discord and crypto projects.

I'm not just about Discord, though—I'm also big into blockchain tech and always looking for ways to mix it with Discord apps. This combo of skills lets me build stuff that's not just useful, but also cutting-edge and secure.

I'm a big believer in community and working together. So, if you've got an idea for a custom bot, a blockchain integration, or anything else Discord-related, I'm up for the challenge. Let's chat on Discord and bring your ideas to life!
